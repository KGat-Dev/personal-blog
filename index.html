Hi, I'm Ken!

Welcome to my corner of the internet. A place to record my thoughts and to connect with curious, thoughtful people along the way.

I enjoy solving problems, philosophizing, and desgining minimal interiors. I tend to collect hobbies, learning deeply before moving on. Following curiosity has led me through seemingly unrelated fields: robotics through mechanical engineering, the human body and physical training, construction management, and more recently, media through writing, coding, and DJing.

Here I collect thoughts on building things (structures, skills, understanding) and the threads I've found moving between them.
